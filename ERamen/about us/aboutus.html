What Is ERamen?

ERamen is established in Hiroshima, Japan in 1910 by Tsuda Family. We are well-known for our handmade organic flour ramen noodle. Through the time, we have expanded our area of serving to overseas countries such as Singapore as our first shop overseas. And in 2015, we opened our first shop in Jakarta, Indonesia.

Our History

1910

Very First Store

Established ERamen first store in Fukuyama, Hiroshima City by the founder Kenjiro Tsuda

1945

Unlucky Experience

Due to the catastrophic effects of the heinous atomic bombings by the U.S., leaving our Fukuyama store destroyed

1955

The Raise

We raised and recovered from the situation and built up our Fukuyama store again

1960

Opened Dotonbori Store

Opened our first store in Osaka region, placed in Dotonbori, led by one of Tsuda's family member

1971

Opened Motomachi Store

Opened our first store in Kobe region, placed in Motomachi

1980

70th Years Anniversary

Opened our first Yokohama store, placed in Isezakicho to celebrate our 70th years anniversary

1985

Opened Shinjuku Store

Opened our first store in Tokyo region, placed in Shinjuku

1987

Opened Rokujigen Store

Opened our second store in Tokyo region, placed in Rokujigen

2010

100th Years Anniversary

Opened our first overseas store in Singapore, placed in Tanjong Pagar by Tsuda's family descendant as 100th years anniversary

2015

First Indonesia Store

Opened our second overseas store in Indonesia, placed in Menteng

Our Vision

To create a one-of-a-kind ramen experience that tastes like welcoming someone to home for our customers by using high-quality ingredients, unique flavor combinations, and exceptional service.

Our Mission

To use the highest quality ingredients that carefully selected for their flavor and freshness, and by creating unique flavor combinations that showcase the best of traditional and modern ramen. To provide exceptional service to ensure that every customer feels welcome and valued, and that they leave our shop with a memorable experience.
